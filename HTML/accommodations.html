Where to Stay in Dublin

Explore the best places to stay in Dublin, from luxury hotels to cozy hostels.

🏨 Luxury Hotels

Indulge at iconic stays like The Shelbourne or The Merrion, where elegance meets history. Expect spa days, fine dining, and next-level comfort.

🏩 Boutique & Mid Range Stays

Modern design and central locations — try The Dean or The Alex for stylish stays without the luxury price tag.

🏨 Budget-Friendly Hotels

From pod-style beds to party vibes, Generator Dublin and Jacobs Inn are perfect for the sociable traveler.

🏡 Bed & Breakfasts

Stay like a local with friendly hosts and home-cooked Irish breakfasts. B&Bs offer charm and warmth in quiet residential areas.

📍Neighborhood Guide

City Centre

Close to everything, therefore perfect for first-time visitors.

Temple Bar

Nightlife central with vibrant streets and pubs.

Ballsbridge

Leafy, upscale, and great for a peaceful retreat.

Howth/Dalkey

Coastal views, seafood, and charming harbors just outside the city.
